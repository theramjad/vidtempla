Questions

Things people ask

What exactly does this do?

VidTempla gives your AI tools (like Claude or Cursor) safe access to your YouTube channel. They can update descriptions, manage playlists, reply to comments, and more.

Is my YouTube account safe?

Yes. You choose what your AI can do by creating read-only or read-write keys. You can revoke access anytime, and every change is logged with full undo history.

What AI tools work with this?

Any tool that supports MCP (like Claude Desktop, Cursor, or Windsurf) connects directly. Everything else can use the REST API with a simple key.

Do I need to write code?

Not at all. If you use an MCP-compatible AI tool, it connects in a couple of clicks. The API is there for developers who want more control.

Can I manage multiple channels?

Yes. Free gets you one channel, Pro gives you three, and Business has no limit. Each channel gets its own set of templates and settings.

What if my AI messes something up?

Every description change is saved with version history. You can undo any change with one click and go back to exactly how it was before.

Is it open source?

Yes, fully open source. You can self-host it or use our hosted version. The code is on GitHub for anyone to review, fork, or contribute to.

What happens if I cancel?

Your YouTube channel stays exactly as it is. We never touch your channel without your AI making an explicit request. Your data exports are always available.
